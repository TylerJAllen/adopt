Sewer Pals

The #1 place to adopt a hero

Name: Leonardo

Nickname: Leo

Leonardo is the oldest of his brothers and the leader, wears a Blue bandana and wields two katana swords. Leo is well known for being smart, strategic, and obedient to his Father/Sensei Master Splinter.

Name: Raphael

Nickname: Raph

Raphael is the second oldest of his brothers and is driven by his anger. He sports a Red bandana and is a master of his Sai’s. Raph is well known for being strong, caring, and hot headed.

Name: Donatello

Nickname: Donnie

Donatello is the third born and is very intelligent. He wears a Purple Bandana and uses a six-foot Bo Staff (Naginata). Donnie is well known for being nervous, astute, and a tech guru.

Name: Michelangelo

Nickname: Mikey

Michelangelo is the youngest and is adored by his brothers, even when he provokes them otherwise. He flaunts an Orange bandana and uses Nunchaku (Nunchuks). Mikey is known for being funny, fast, and high-flying.

Name: Master Splinter

Nickname: Master P

Master Splinter is the father and sensei who raised and taught the Ninja Turtles. Previously led Japan’s Foot Clan before an altercation with Oroku Saki which cost him his family. He fled to America against his own will and was mutated to a giant rat in New York, he then met his new turtle family.
